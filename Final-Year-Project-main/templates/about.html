About us!

Company Name : Shwet Multimetals
Establishment year : 1970

Our Story

Welcome to Shwet Multimetals, your trusted source for high-quality metal products. With a heritage dating back to 1970, we have proudly served the metal industry for over 53 years. Our journey from inception to becoming a respected name in the industry has been a testament to our unwavering dedication, commitment to quality, and passion for metal excellence.

Our Product

At Shwet Multinational Company, we are passionate about providing top-grade metal products to meet your industrial and commercial needs. We specialize in the following key product categories:

Nichrome Strip:

Our Nichrome strips are known for their exceptional resistance properties, making them an ideal choice for a wide range of heating applications. We offer a variety of specifications to suit your unique requirements.

Titanium Sheets:

Our Titanium sheets are crafted to deliver the perfect blend of strength and lightness. Whether you require sheets for aerospace, medical, or industrial purposes, our high-quality Titanium products have you covered.

Tungsten Vacuum Filaments:

We offer precision-engineered Tungsten vacuum filaments designed to meet the demands of vacuum deposition processes. Our filaments are known for their reliability and consistent performance.
